About Us

Welcome to our little slice of Melbourne in Maryborough. We're more than just a coffee shop – we're a community hub where every cup tells a story.

Our beans are locally roasted to perfection, and our space is a carefully curated collection of retro finds, from vintage VCRs to mismatched cups that each have their own character.

We believe in creating a space where everyone feels at home, whether you're here for your morning ritual or to catch up with friends.

Pay It Forward Coffees This Month

15 coffees waiting for whoever needs one

Our pay-it-forward board is a community initiative where you can buy a coffee for someone who might need it. Each month, we aim to provide 50 coffees to those who could use a little pick-me-up.
